ABOUT ME

I started this website with the intention to tell my story and share it with the World in order for me to relate with the youngsters out there.
This is to give comfort and shelter to our own stories.I want to show you how much struggle, happiness, and faults we have in common.
I guess the bottomline is we're separated, but not alone...only if we choose to see it. I may not be that superstar or that model you look up to,
but I am that teenager who once found themselve in your shoes. Let's tell that story!

STORY

---Err'day life---

The oily, spit filled , uneven roads of Langa, decorated with cigarette buds and moles.
Houses and shacks neighbouring each other, sponsored by crime.The Huge blinding
wave of smoke coming from the fires set alight by the aunts trying to sell sheep heads
that we never even bother to greet when passing by-and an object of demise, virtually
stuck on the hand of the ghetto youth, that feel's motivated to support the somali spazas,
buying lighters day in day out. Yes, these are the streets where I reside, and I "was" that
ghetto teen. I also used to believe that change was for the fancy small population, that has
nothing else to do with there lives. But not so deep inside, I longed for that change that I
hate speeched so much, and peer stigma wouldn't let me get a single taste of it. So there
I was...propagandered to know I had no choice but to pretend I'm delighted with the person
I am. I had the perfect support to help me go down life's drain, sometimes I could hear there
cheers.People I thought were my friends, and later on I realised they were just fans...

---Personal Changes---

...Now, a lot had changed.Looking at my mother in the eyes, she's looking at me like a
new person. Someone in me told me slow down...the pain won't go away with the
changes. All I innocently wanted was to get away from all my problems, instead I built
them a mansion inside and they sure had a lot of reletives. The escape plan was
emphetamins and a well scaled ecstacy for the weekend, and so my problems escalated...
Realising this was the problem did not make things any better, and I felt guilty at the fact
that I was starting to enjoy it. It felt so good when I'm with friends, but felt so bad when I'm at home.
I started felling uncumfortable in my own home, talking to my mother seemed to get harder...
That's when I realised Inga was left in the past, and Stilo(friends nickname) took over. I tried
convincing myself that there's nothing wrong with the person I've become,
but I just couldn't. The more I tried, the more I missed myself, my clean self...

---I Change my Mind---

...I Changed My Mind. Everything started to seem easier, but not quit how I preffered them to be.
So I started with my own home.Changing my attitude towards my family, and it actually made me
feel better about being my mother's child. I changed from talking, to listening.I listened to the the
people who I claimed to care about, and tried my best to adjust to what they preffered to smile at.
I noticed a change in my inner circle,and even gained greetings from people whom I never even
knew they know my name.
